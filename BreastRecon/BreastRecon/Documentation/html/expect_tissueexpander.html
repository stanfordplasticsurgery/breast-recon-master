What to Expect After Mastectomy &
Tissue Expander Placement
Post Op Day #0 - the day of surgery
  • Regular diet
  • Out of bed to chair and ambulating
  • Voiding on your own, no foley (urinary catheter)
  • IV fluids
  • Breast binder or Ace bandage over surgical site (surgeon preference)
  • Incentive Spirometry x 10 per hour while awake
  • Oral pain medication
  • Drain teaching by nurse
Post Op Day #1
  • Regular diet
  • Out of bed to chair and ambulating
  • IV fluids discontinued
  • Breast binder or Ace bandage over surgical site
  • Oral pain medication
  • Incentive Spirometry x 10 per hour while awake
  • Drain teaching by nurse
  • Possible discharge to home
