Breast Reduction

Combined breast reconstruction with contralateral breast reduction: For women with large breasts, contralateral breast reduction helps decrease the volume of the unaffected breast to match the size of the reconstructed breast. Breast reductions are generally performed using a Wise pattern skin resection or a vertical technique. If a Wise pattern skin resection technique is used, the scar looks like an inverted T extending down from the nipple and horizontally across the lower fold of the breast. In the vertical technique, the scar is a vertical line extending from the nipple to the lower breast fold (no horizontal component).
