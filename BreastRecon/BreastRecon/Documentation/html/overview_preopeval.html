Pre-operative Evaluation

The preoperative evaluation begins with a consultation appointment with your surgical oncologist and plastic surgeon to discuss your cancer surgery and reconstructive options. Once a decision to proceed with surgery is made, additional work-ups and assessments are done to prepare you for your surgery, including the following:

  • Preoperative blood work, urinalysis, chest X-ray and EKG as required
  • Additional preoperative imaging studies may be recommended by your surgeon
  • Consult with your primary care physician about whether you should continue to take your regular medications or supplements prior to your surgery
  • If you have medical conditions that may affect how you will tolerate anesthesia or surgery, medical clearance from the primary care physician and medical specialist may be necessary
  • Appointment with the anesthesiologist to go over types and risks of anesthesia
  • Preoperative teachings by the nurse coordinator who will review the important preoperative and postoperative instructions
