Medications to Avoid Before and After Surgery

The following drugs either contain and/or have undesirable side effects that may affect your surgery (abnormal bleeding and bruising). These drugs should not be taken for 1-2 weeks prior to your surgery and for 1 week following surgery, unless specifically instructed to take them by your surgeon.

PLEASE INFORM YOUR DOCTOR/NURSE IF YOU ARE CURRENTLY TAKING ANY MEDICATIONS ON THE LIST BELOW.
** ALL VITAMINS
** ALL AND ANY HERBAL PREPARATIONS
In tablet, liquid, or drink form, including Gingko, St. John’s Wort, Grape Seed, Tea, Echinacea, Sobe’s beverages, Red Bull, Juice Plus, Booster Juices, Flax Seed
