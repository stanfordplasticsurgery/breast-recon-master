Anatomy
The goals of an aesthetic breast reconstruction are:
  1. restore the anatomical landmarks of the breast
  2. create an appropriate breast size for a given body type
  3. recreate a natural breast shape
Anatomical landmarks

The upper, medial and lateral borders of the breast and the inframammary fold make up the footprint or anatomical landmarks that are unique to each patient.

Breast size

The size of the breast is determined by its volume and the skin envelope. An implant or autologous tissue transfer provides the volume. The skin envelope can be adjusted, such as in a breast lift, to change the breast size.

Breast shape

The overall breast shape is affected by a combination of factors, including the breast landmarks, volume and skin envelope.
