Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Post Surgical Dressings
Outer gauze dressings can be removed 24 hours post operatively. Steri strips stay in place until seen in post op visits. It is okay to shower over them 3 days post op. Usually they start to curl or fall off on their own 7-10 days after surgery. During your visit, the provider may clear you to take off the rest of the steri-strips in the shower.
