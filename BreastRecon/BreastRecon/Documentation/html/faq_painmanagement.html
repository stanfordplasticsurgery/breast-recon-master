Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Pain Management
You will be given a prescription for pain medication at the time of discharge from the hospital such as Norco (Vicodin) or Percocet. Please take as prescribed. These medications require a hand written prescription by your provider to hand carry to your pharmacy. Please allow 1-2 day lead time for refills so that a provider can write this prescription for you. If you live a distance away, your local physician may need to do this. We anticipate that you will wean off your pain medications within 2-3 weeks of your surgery. Please call for advise on how to taper off your pain medications.

If you are having breast reconstruction with tissue expanders, you will be given a prescription for Valium or a medication that will help to relax your muscles. The pectoralis muscle can cramp or spasm as you go through the tissue expansion process.

Do not drive while taking either pain medications, muscle relaxants or any medication that causes sedation.
